About Us

Headmaster Simphiwe Khuze
Background

The 13th of March 1972 saw the birth of Mr. Simphiwe Khuze. He was raised in Middleburg, Eastern Cape, where he was born. His love for teaching began during his high school years at Archie Velile Senior Secondary, while he attended his primary school years at Mchobololo Primary School in the Eastern Cape. He graduated from high school in 1988, moved to the Western Cape to continue his education, and in 1994 he earned a bachelor's degree in education from the University of Western Cape (UWC).

Experience

April 2004 through December 2017 He began working as a history teacher and vice principal at Manzomthombo Senior Secondary School in Mfuleni, Cape Town, following his graduation from UWC.
He worked at WCED as an Internal Moderator, Chief Marker, and History NSC Marker.
In January2018, He was appointed principal of Mzamomtsha Primary School and continues to be so.
Simphiwe Khuze, the headmaster, leaves us with these challenging remarks. The quote "If I can control my mind, I can control anything" is attributed to H H Sri Sri Ravishankar.

Mzamomtsha Primary School Biography



In the Cape Town neighborhood of Driftsand, the Mzamomtsha Primay school opened its doors in 2008. Originally intended for children in grades 1 through 7, the school is now admitting kids in higher grades due to the significant population expansion (births and migration). Students are permitted up until grade 12 (the matric year).

The Mzamomtsha Primary School is experiencing a number of issues as a result of the rapid growth. In order to accommodate the ongoing increase, there are not enough staff members or classrooms. There are 20 teachers working at the school, which has 700 students.
